Managing My Workload

My committment to not being a soggy liability.

In order to be a good worker I am going to try to get good sleep, spend some time outside each day, and not work more than 8 hours per day if I can help it. This will mean it is crucial to put my headphones in and my head down when I need to work. I will use my timer when I get stuck and ensure I spend no more than 20 minutes trying to solve a problem before I ask for help.


When I notice that I am stressed I am going to physically step away from my desk. I will do some breathing exercises or starjumps or even go for a walk and talk to someone. I will ask for help if this happens multiple times on the same problem.
